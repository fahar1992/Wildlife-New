About NorthPrairie

Technical support built around clear methods and useful deliverables.

NorthPrairie is an independent consulting practice led by Dr. Fahar Ibtisham, DVM, MSc and PhD.

A practical bridge between science, field work and project decisions.

The work focuses on translating complex wildlife, field or research needs into plans, maps, methods, SOPs, reports and other materials that teams can use.

Relevant background includes veterinary biomedical science, applied research, field operations, wildlife health, study design, technical documentation and stakeholder-facing reporting.

How NorthPrairie works

  • Starts with the project decision and the useful deliverable
  • Uses documented methods, realistic assumptions and clear limitations
  • Keeps files organized for internal review and handover
  • Accepts work only where scope, availability and independence can be confirmed

Scope and independence

NorthPrairie does not provide legal advice, veterinary clinical care or emergency response. Inquiries are screened before acceptance to confirm that the proposed work can be completed independently and appropriately.
