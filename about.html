NorthPrairie / About

The practice

Science, field experience and technical clarity—connected.

NorthPrairie is an independent wildlife and ecology technical consultancy led by Dr. Fahar Ibtisham, DVM, MSc and PhD.

How the work is approached

Turn complex information into clear, useful decisions.

NorthPrairie focuses on the practical bridge between scientific reasoning, field operations and project decisions. The output is not generic advice; it is a defined plan, system, map, SOP, report or other material designed for the next step in a real project.

  • Technical depthAdvanced training in veterinary biomedical science and applied research.
  • Applied perspectiveExperience with wildlife health, field operations, study design and technical documentation.
  • Useful handoverOrganized files, clear limitations and materials that support review and implementation.

Scope & independence

Defined work. Clear boundaries.

NorthPrairie does not provide legal advice, veterinary clinical care or emergency response. Each inquiry is screened before acceptance to confirm scope, availability and the ability to complete the work independently and appropriately.
