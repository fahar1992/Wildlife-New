GIS and study design that make field effort count.

From location data to a usable decision framework

NorthPrairie supports project teams that need a documented approach to site screening, spatial analysis, survey design, data collection and map-ready communication.

  • Study designObjectives, sampling units, timing, effort, constraints and analysis-ready data plans.
  • GIS mappingClear map packages, documented inputs and spatial summaries for reports and decisions.
  • Habitat and site screeningStructured review of land cover, terrain, access, observations and relevant project context.
  • Data organizationCoordinate standards, field data structures, metadata and reproducible workflows.
Good GIS is not only a map. It records assumptions, sources, limitations and methods so another reviewer can understand how the output was produced.
